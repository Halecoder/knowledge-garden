## obsidian自带 callout

[Use callouts - Obsidian Help](https://help.obsidian.md/How+to/Use+callouts)

用 co- 前缀，通过 [[various complements]] 变量自动补全插件自动触发。

目前全部的callout字段：

- note
- abstract, summary, tldr
- info, todo
- tip, hint, important
- success, check, done
- question, help, faq
- warning, caution, attention
- failure, fail, missing
- danger, error
- bug
- example
- quote, cite
