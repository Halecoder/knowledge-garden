### 最近编辑

```dataview
table WITHOUT ID file.link AS "File",file.mtime as "修改时间"
from !"模板" and !"kanban"
sort file.mtime desc
limit 6
```

### 三天内创建的笔记

```dataview
table file.ctime as 创建时间
from ""
where date(today) - file.ctime <=dur(3 days)
sort file.ctime desc
limit 6
```
